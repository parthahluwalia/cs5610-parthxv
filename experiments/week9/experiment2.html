About the experiment

This experiment demonstrates the analysis that has been performed based on which a phrase adjective and sentiment score has been calculated.

The analysis shows the tweet that has been analyzed about the subject, the words matched in the AFINN word-list, the positive words, the negative words and the score that has been calculated. The average comparative score calculated is shown at the top of the Analysis.
