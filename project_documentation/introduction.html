Introduction

Sentiments Social Network is a Sentiment Analysis Web Application that takes in in a subject and subsequenty fetches the tweets related to that subject from Twitter. Each of these tweets is then fed to the Sentiment constituent of the app, which is an AFINN word-list powered sentiment analysis module. This constituent of the app evaluates the scores for the respective tweets, and the upshot is a comparative mean score generated by the app for the gathered tweets.

Before we move on, you can take a look at the Sentiments Social Network. The code for this has been open sourced at my GitHub repository for CS5610.


A Gist of Available Features and Pages

Twitter Sign In
The App is built on Twitter, so we support only strategy to sign into the App and access the Twitter network - through Passport Strategy for Twitter and Twitter OAuth Model!
Subject Sentiment Module
One of the robust modules of the app, that allows you assess the sentiments of your subjects that you provide as a text input. It can be any phrase, the application will go through the last 15 tweets posted about the subject, extracting the subjective information through computational linguistics and text analysis, and generate a sentiment for the subject of concern. Try Northeastern Huskies, and find out how they are doing now!
People In the Network
Search for people in the network by typing their name / twitter screen_name, and the app will go through the twitter users and show the relevant matched users, along with analyzing their recent tweets for evaluating their sentiments, that can be seen on their profile page.
User Account
Upon logging in, a user is able to go to their dedicated profile page describing them and where they can watch the news feed of people they are monitoring or the subjects they have bookmarked, and post a tweet to suggest activities on their Twitter network.
Twitter Friends
This represents the friends of a person on Twitter. This tab is available on the current logged in user's profile page as well as any other user's profile page navigated to in the network.
Twitter Followers
This represents the followers of a person on Twitter. This tab is available on the current logged in user's profile page as well as any other user's profile page navigated to in the network.
Monitor Users
A vigorous feature of the app is to monitor users so that their sentiments could be put to watch by a user and is made available to the user as a news feed. All that is needed to monitor a user is that they should be on Twitter network. Clicking "Monitor" button by going to their profile will make you watch their sentiments as a news feed on your home page. The most recent analysis of any user can also be viewed by going to their profile page. Any user can be unmonitored from your watch my clicking on the "Monitoring" button on their page, or from the list of your monitored users made available to you.
Bookmark Subjects
Another great feature of the app is to allow a logged in user to bookmark his subjects of interest so that their sentiments are also available to the user in their news feed. The news feed will contain the sentiments of these subjects by analyzing last 15 tweets posted about them. Any subject can be removed from your bookmarks by clicking on the respective unbookmark icon.
Submitting Queries
Any suggestions / feedback could be submitted by a user through the "Contact Us" page built on MailGun transport of NodeMailer.
