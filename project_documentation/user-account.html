Bookmarking Subjects of Interest

A user could bookmark his subject of interest and view thier sentiments on thier feeds page anytime they want. A subject is bookmarked from the Subject Sentiment Module.

Bookmark Icon

In the Subject Sentiment Module, the bookmark icon will appear only if a user has logged in. The user visits the page, analyze their subject of interst, and a bookmark icon appears upon clicking which the application records the bookmarked subject for the respective user.

Lets search for New Delhi and bookmark it.

On clicking the Bookmark icon, a message appears "The respective subject has been successfully bookmarked", if the same subject has not already been bookmarked and being watched by the user.

The bookmarked subjects could be navigated to and viewed any time from the left navbar of current user's account page.

The bookmarks will be displayed as:


Any of the bookmarks' sentiments could be viewed by clicking on them.


Since there could be many tweets posted about a subject by anyone on Twitter, the analysis concern is much wider here as compared to a single user. Hence, at any time, we analyze last 15 tweets posted about a subject to evaluate their feelings. The analysis can be viewed by clicking on the "Analysis" button.


Also, the subjects could be unbookmarked anytime by clicking on the red Unbookmark button next to the bookmarked subjects.
