Subject Sentiments Module

If a user wishes to see the sentiments of a subject, the subject could be entered as a text input, and "Analyze Sentiments" button could be clicked. Last 15 tweets (configurable in the app) posted about the subject are gathered, and each of the tweet is fed to our sentiments module. The sentiment module assigns a polarity to each tweet, extracting the subjective information through computational linguistics and text analysis.
