Abstract

Sentiments Social Network has been perceived and developed as a full MEAN Stack Application to assess and analyze the tweets on a subject. The network is social in the sense that it has been built on Twitter.

This online social networking service uses text analysis and computational linguistics to identify and extract subjective information in source materials and helps you analyze the sentiments of your subject - it can be people on your twitter network, locations or phrases.

This Sentiment Analysis App is disparate from the conventional Sentiment Analysis methods in a way that I have utilized assessing the sentiments with a social networking service and tried to make it available to the populace for riveting functions it can be put to use.

It addresses so many questions - how a place is doing, how a person is feeling, can a company improve business and what are the target areas where it can improve business, psychology of a person, weather or not a person shows some behavioral tendencies, online personalities, are there any social media / media benefits - that could be answered based on our Sentiments Social Network.
