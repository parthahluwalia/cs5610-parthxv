Welcome Page

The Home page (first view) of the App contains a short description about the core module of our social network, a Twitter Sign In link that allows a user to login through Twitter and the links to navigate to the Subject Module of the app, people search in the network, contact us page, profile page (if a user is logged in) and logout button.


Upon clicking the Twitter Sign In button, a user is directed to the "Login through Twitter portal" with various app persmissions being displayed.


When a user enters his valid credentials in the portal, and they are authenticated, they are redirected back to their home page with "Profile" and "Logout" buttons in the navbar.
